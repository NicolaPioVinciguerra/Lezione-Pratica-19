Karlotta Freier

Karlotta Freier is an accomplished Brooklyn-based
artist with a background in illustration.
In 2021, she graduated from the MFA Illustration
as Visual Essay program at the School of Visual Arts.
In addition to her creative pursuits, Karlotta also
dedicates her time to sharing her knowledge with the
next generation of artists. She has taught
illustration at the China Academy of Art and spoken
as a guest speaker at universities across the United States.
Freier's work has also been exhibited in a variety of
solo and group exhibitions, as well as festivals worldwide.

Her achievements have been recognized by
the Art Directors Club, which awarded her a Cube
in 2018 and a Pencil for Best Illustration Portfolio in 2021.
